Patagonia Argentina

Data

Area: 1,043,000 km²

Population: Approximately 2 million

Capital: Neuquén City

Languages: Spanish

Currency: Argentine Peso (ARS)

Time Zone: Argentina Time (ART) UTC-3

Calling Code: +54

Internet TLD: .ar

Weather

Temperature: Varies by region; averages range from -5°C to 25°C (23°F to 77°F)

Conditions: Diverse; includes arid, temperate, and cold regions

Wind: Generally moderate, with stronger winds in exposed areas

Wind Chill: Can feel significantly colder, especially in winter
